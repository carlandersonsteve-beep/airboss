# GroundCore Project Context

## Mission
GroundCore is a digital operations platform for FBOs.

Its job is to help the operation run cleanly from arrival through service completion.

## Product Goal
Turn this into a real, reliable, usable operations system for:
- ramp
- front desk
- management

## Focus
This is a standalone system.
Do not optimize it around flight tracker integration right now.

## Priorities
- operational clarity
- reliability
- maintainability
- good workflow
- simple user experience

## Users
- ramp crew
- front desk staff
- management

## Constraints
- keep it simple
- keep it fast
- keep it usable on iPad/mobile
- avoid unnecessary complexity
